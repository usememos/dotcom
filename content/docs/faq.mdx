## Is Memos open source?

Yes. Memos is released under the MIT License.

## Where is my data stored?

Data is stored in your own database (SQLite, MySQL, or PostgreSQL). Attachments can be stored in the database, on the local filesystem, or in S3-compatible storage.

## Does Memos have an API?

Yes. Memos provides REST and gRPC APIs. See the [API reference](/docs/api).

## Is there a demo?

Yes. You can try the live demo at https://demo.usememos.com.
